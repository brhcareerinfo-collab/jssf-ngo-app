# Jeevan Setu Sahayata Foundation (JSSF)

## 🌍 About
Jeevan Setu Sahayata Foundation (JSSF) is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving humanity through healthcare, education, social welfare, environmental protection, disaster relief, women empowerment, youth development, and community service.

Our mission is to create a healthier, educated, and self-reliant society by connecting people with opportunities and support.

---

## 🎯 Mission
To serve humanity with transparency, dedication, and innovation while improving the quality of life for underprivileged communities.

## 👁 Vision
To become one of India's most trusted social organizations by empowering people through health, education, and sustainable development.
